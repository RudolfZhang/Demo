Administrators can delegate Group Policy administrative tasks to other users. These users do not have to be domain administrators; they can be users that are granted certain rights to GPOs.

For example, different users could be granted different rights to a GPO.

  • Manager - Tasked with performing reporting and analysis duties.
  • Help Desk – Tasked with editing the GPO.
  • Developers – Tasked with overseeing the creation of Windows Management Instrumentation (WMI) filters.

You can delegate the following Group Policy tasks independently.

  • Creating GPOs.
  • Editing GPOs.
  • Managing Group Policy links for a site, domain, or OU.
  • Performing Group Policy Modeling analyses on a given domain or OU.
  • Reading Group Policy Results data for objects in a given domain or OU.
  • Creating WMI filters in a domain.
