Group policy preferences are similar to group policy settings in that they apply configurations to the user or computer. However, there are several differences:

  • Preferences are not enforced. However, you can configure preferences to reapply automatically.
  • Preferences can use item-level targeting. Item-level targeting enables you to target individual preferences within a GPO to specific users or computers.
