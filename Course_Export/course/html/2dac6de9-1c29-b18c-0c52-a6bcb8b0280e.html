edX FAQ

If this is your first edX course the Help Center answers a lot of questions about the edX learning experience. Which browsers are supported? What if I can't remember my password? How do I get credit for my course? Take a moment to read the questions and bookmark the page.

We recommend you check out DemoX – a fun, interactive course that’s designed to help you explore the edX learning experience.

Layout of the course

To get started, click on the **Course **tab at the top of the page. This course is organized into modules. Each module contains one or more topics. Use the Navigation Bar at the top to move through the topic pages. Notice the Bookmark feature.

Discussion Board

This course includes a discussion board where you can post course issues, technical questions, or general help items. Click the Discussion tab to begin interacting with other students. When you can, help other students with their questions.
